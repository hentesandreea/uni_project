Art Museum of Timisoara

The Art Museum of Timișoara (Muzeul de Artă) is an art museum in Timișoara that holds a collection of 90 works and personal belongings of the painter Corneliu Baba. Furthermore, the museum holds three big collections: contemporary, decorative and European art collections. including collections of Romanian paintings, patintings from Banat and religious paintings. On the ground floor, Muzeul de Artă organises temporary exhibitions.
